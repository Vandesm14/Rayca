# What is RAYCA?

RAYCA (Random ASCII You Can Admire) provides the best automatically curated collection of procedurally generated plain text art for your eyes to enjoy. Every piece goes through an extensively tested algorithm purpose-made to create practically identical art.

We supply pseudo-random tokens produced from these art pieces that you can own a copy of and view on this site exclusively — be sure to collect yours while they're still available.

The road ahead seems prosperous, with the fever dream of a plain text metaverse on the horizon, founded on a pyramid of profits the likes of which we have never seen before.
